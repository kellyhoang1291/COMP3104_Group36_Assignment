COMP 3104 DevOps course
the devops class is meant to prepare individuals that would like to work in IT related jobs and teach
about the processes and tools involved in deploying services and products.
the goals is to facilitate the interaction between IT fields as servers, web apps and infrastructor.
It is also equipped with organizational tools and plans meant to enhance the speed and productivity in fixing issues, adapting to changes, 
deploying products and so on.

In this course we will be learning about Amazon Web Services, Ansible, Docker and
Travis CI.
