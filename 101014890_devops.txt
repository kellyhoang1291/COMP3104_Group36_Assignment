DEVOPS comp3104 is a course taught at georgebrown college. It encompasses many different
devops concepts in the domain of CI/CD. It's purpose to introduce students
to important topics in devops, such as continous intergration, continuous delivery, 
containers and the practical application of these topics. It also requires 
the use of git and github which gives students really important hands on
experience with VCS. It also gives students a preview of what it's like working
with a team and coordinating via github workflows. 


Devops will provide students the skills and knowledge that will 
enable them to become productive programmers, or even a first stepping stone
for a career as a Devops engineer.
